How to make lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onions and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

How to layer the lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan
